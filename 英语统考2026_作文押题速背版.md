> 只背三套主命题，再补一个图表模板。
> 
> 每篇都按同一套路写：开头表态 + 两个理由 + 结尾总结。

## 先背通用骨架

```text
Paragraph 1: introduce the topic and state your opinion.
Paragraph 2: give reason 1 and reason 2.
Paragraph 3: conclude and restate your opinion.
```

```text

Some people believe ..., while others argue ....
From my perspective, ... is the better choice.
```

## 1. 教育 / 升学 / 实用能力

高概率题目：

- Why do so many people pursue graduate studies?
- Should universities focus more on practical skills?

立意一句：

- 高等教育不只是拿文凭，更是提升知识、能力和就业竞争力。

可背模板：

```text
Nowadays, higher education has become a heated topic. Some people believe universities should focus on academic subjects, while others argue that practical skills are more useful.
From my perspective, both are important, but practical skills should not be ignored.
First, they help students adapt to real life and future work. Second, they can improve problem-solving ability and communication skills. Last but not least, they make graduates more competitive in the job market.
To sum up, universities should combine academic study with practical training.
```

背诵版范文：

```text
Nowadays, more and more people choose to pursue graduate studies. Some people think this is a smart way to improve their future, while others believe it may cost too much time and money. From my perspective, further study is worthwhile.

First, graduate study helps people gain deeper knowledge and stronger professional skills. Second, it offers more chances to find a better job and broaden one's horizons. Last but not least, it can make people more confident and more independent.

To sum up, although graduate study is not the only road to success, it is still a valuable choice for many people.
```

记忆关键词：education, skills, job, horizons。

## 2. 科技 / AI / 机器替代

高概率题目：

- Can machine translation fully replace human translators?
- Will AI change our life for the better or for the worse?

立意一句：

- 技术是工具，不是替代人类判断、情感和创造力的答案。

可背模板：

```text
Nowadays, there is a heated debate over whether technology can fully replace human beings.
Some people hold that it can because it is fast and efficient. However, I do not think so.
First, technology is good at handling simple and repetitive tasks. Second, human beings are better at understanding culture, tone and emotions. Last but not least, complex problems still require human judgement.
In short, technology can only be a tool, but it cannot completely replace human beings.
```

背诵版范文：

```text
Nowadays, there is a heated debate over whether machine translation can fully replace human translators. Some people believe machines are faster and cheaper, while others insist that human translators are still irreplaceable. I agree with the second view.

First, machine translation can save time in simple and repetitive tasks. Second, human translators are better at understanding culture, tone and inner feelings. Last but not least, human judgement is still necessary when the text is complex or ambiguous.

In short, machine translation is useful, but it cannot completely replace human translators.
```

记忆关键词：tool, language, culture, judgement。

## 3. 价值观 / 竞争 / 心理压力

高概率题目：

- Is winning what counts most in our life?
- How should we deal with competition and stress?

立意一句：

- 赢很重要，但健康、成长、关系和内心平静更重要。

可背模板：

```text
Nowadays, competition has become a hot topic in our daily life.
Some people think winning is the most important thing, while others believe personal growth matters more.
As far as I am concerned, I prefer the latter view.
First, winning may encourage people to work hard, but it should not be the only goal. Second, excessive competition often brings stress and anxiety. Last but not least, a meaningful life depends on values, health and relationships, not only on results.
To sum up, winning is important, but it is not everything.
```

背诵版范文：

```text
Nowadays, there exists a heated discussion about whether winning matters most in our life. Some people think victory is the key to success, while others believe peace of mind and personal growth are more important. I tend to support the latter view.

First, winning may encourage people to work hard, but it should not be the only goal. Second, excessive competition often brings stress and anxiety. Last but not least, a meaningful life depends on values, health and relationships, not only on results.

To sum up, winning is important, but it is not everything.
```

记忆关键词：winning, growth, stress, balance。

## 4. 图表题备用模板

如果 2026 继续出图表题，直接套这个开头：

```text
The chart clearly shows that ... rose steadily from ... to ....
Overall, the trend indicates that ....
There are several reasons for this change.
First, ... . Second, ... .
In the long run, ... will continue to play an important role.
```

图表题最稳的写法不是抠细节，而是写清：

- 上升或下降的趋势
- 原因
- 影响
- 总结
